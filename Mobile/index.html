Learning
is just the Beginning
At Charlsedale School, you'll join a rearing and collaborative community where students of all backgrounds champion and help each other.
Students aren't just taught. They are propelled and guided to grasp challenges, discover and develop the particular gifts that are theirs and prepare them to lead lives of constructive service to the world, find their qualities and make their mark.
Our Mission

I want to make it clear out commitment is to help the children develop into happy, secure and confident young people, well equipped to move on to the next tage of their education and to achieve their maximum potential as functional members of society.
